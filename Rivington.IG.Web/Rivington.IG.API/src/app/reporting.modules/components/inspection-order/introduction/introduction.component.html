Thank you for choosing Rivington Partners for your homeowner’s insurance needs. We look forward to a long relationship between you and our company.


We are pleased to be providing you with this specialized Risk Management Report, which includes the summarization of the results of our recent visit to your home.

There are three parts to our report:

The Replacement Cost Valuation of your home. Our inspector has taken the information gathered from you in our visit, combined with observations of the building materials used to construct your home to determine a comprehensive replacement cost analysis.

The Loss Mitigation Recommendations are a list of actions recommended and/or required to proactively protect your home from future losses.

The Wildfire Review is an assessment of your home’s susceptibility to wildfire loss, and a list of recommended and/or required actions for you to take, to minimize your risk and keep your home protected.

It is our hope that this report will help you and your insurance agent to develop a comprehensive plan of protection for your home and your family.

Our goal in providing you with this report is to make recommendations to you, to help you proactively protect your home from potential losses, and/or to make recommendations to enhance your safety and security.

Please take the opportunity to discuss this report with your insurance agent.
